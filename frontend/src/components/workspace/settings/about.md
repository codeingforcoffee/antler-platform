# 🦌 [About Antler Platform](https://github.com/codeingforcoffee/antler-platform)

> **From Open Source, Back to Open Source**

**Antler Platform** is a super agent workspace built on top of the open-source **[DeerFlow](https://github.com/bytedance/deer-flow)** harness (**D**eep **E**xploration and **E**fficient **R**esearch **Flow**) that researches, codes, and creates.
With the help of sandboxes, memories, tools and skills, it handles
different levels of tasks that could take minutes to hours.
